## Nutter ##
### by Ellie Henderson ###
<br>
Nutter is a display typeface for a nut butter brand. It's curly forms are inspired by the organic shapes of nuts and the creaminess of nut butter dripping from a spoon.
<br><br>
Sketching and more sketching!
